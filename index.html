Model United Nation 2k19

I, on behalf of VTMUN Club of Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science & Technology, am pleased to announce that the inaugural edition of Vel Tech Model United Nations shall take place on 1st, 2nd, and 3rd March 2020.

​ Being a part of the Secretariat has been an absolute honor, and I regret not being able to sit among the Delegates in those temples of decision making. The best parts of every Model United Nations are always the delegates, the front line warriors. People call us by many names - Youth Summits, Mock Parliaments, Diplomacy Conferences - often forgetting that MUNs are much more than that. They are the sparring grounds of future world leaders, they are the battlefields of knowledge and intellect, they are the breeding grounds for warriors in formal suits. ​

Thus, I hereby welcome all who have taken part in the three-day summit and I convey my heartfelt gratitude to all who have given their blood and sweat in making this event a reality. I take pleasure in inviting all others to be audience to this spectacular event and provide their valuable suggestions. Last but not least, nothing would have been a reality without the support and encouragement from Prof. V.S.S. Kumar, respected Vice-Chancellor, Dr. E. Kannan, Director of Campus Affairs and Dr. Mohammed S. Iqbal, our Faculty Advisor.

So, I again welcome you to be a part of Vel Tech Model United Nations 2020 and lay the foundation of a legacy.

Soham,
Secretary General, Vel Tech Model United Nations
